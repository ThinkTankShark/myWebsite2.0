about

I have moved to 3 different countries all by myself half of my life. When I was 16, I moved to Cyprus to pursue my computer science degree, then UK and now US.

Through this journey, I thought about quitting around 1,000 times (probably more than that). But despite all the hardship in adopting different countries’ culture, making friends, learning new languages, I kept going. I’m that person. Once I say I’ll do something, it will happen.

I put that determination to work as a software developer. I'm incredibly passionate about crafting beautiful and intuitive UI / UX to ensure the best possible user interactions.
